passengers during air travel due to close proximity. Crewmembers may be exposed to

has  developed  guidance  for  airlines  for  managing  ill  passengers/crew.  and  reporting

specific  recommendations  aren’t  available,  aircrew  should  follow  the  routine  infection
control guidance for cabin crew and consult with their employer’s occupational health
